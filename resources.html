These resources were incredibly helpful to me during my time abroad!

    Transportation

  • Skyscanner has relatively cheap flights.
  • Ferry Scanner was useful for scheduling ferries in Greece
  • Rail Europe is a site where you can book trains from one city to another

    Communication

  • Google Translate can aid travelers with quick conversations with locals
  • WhatsApp is a platform where you can call and message your friends with different phone plans and in different countries

    Boarding Accommodations

  • Booking.com is useful for finding hotel and flight deals for relatively low prices
  • Hostelworld allows travelers to book private or shared rooms for low prices around the world
