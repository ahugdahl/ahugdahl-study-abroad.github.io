My name is Lexi Hugdahl and I am a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Last spring I had the opportunity to study abroad in Barcelona!

In Spain, I had the absolute pleasure of learning about the Spanish culture and language. I attended business classes with American students and Spanish natives.

My learnings did not stop in the classroom! I was fortunate enough to have traveled to 9 countries during my time in Europe and each new experience broadened my perspective.

I believe every student able to study abroad absolutely should take the leap and experience something new!
